8-bit apes

Apes with attitude. 10,000 pixel primates inspired by the smooth brain humans over at R/Wallstreetbets. Uniquely collectible limited edition characters that include a proof of ownership stored on the Ethereum blockchain.

They are a new wave in the modern CryptoArt movement. 8-bit-apes are "Non-Fungible Tokens" on the Ethereum network and part of the ERC standard that powers most digital art and collectibles.


The 8 bit apes are uniquely hand drawn characters, released in batches. No two are exactly alike and each one of them can be bought and sold on the Ethereum blockchain. They can be purchased from the Opensea marketplace. Via this market you can buy or bid on apes for sale.

See the marketplace instructions below to acquire your very own ape. Follow @millionpoundape on twitter for news and updates of releases.

What 8-bit ape?

The 8-bit-apes follow the original Cryptopunks and are 24x24 pixel art images inspired by the fearless members of Reddit in R/wallstreetbets. Most are chill normal apes, but mixed among the ranks are Space apes, Green apes, Clown apes, WallStreetBets apes and even the odd Cyborg. Every ape has their own unique combination of features, however some are much rarer than others like the zero trait apes.

GOLDEN apes are the rarest of apes and the pure 24-carat-ape (zero trait) is the rarest of all.

WSB apes are identified by the red bandanna.

What all fuss about?

Crypto currency was made famous by the meteroic rise of Bitcoin. It was designed just to transact and store ownership of the Bitcoin token itself, many refer to it as digital gold because it is used as a store of wealth. Ethereum is a successor to Bitcoin which allows computer code to be executed on the blockchain (contracts) and the results of this to be stored forever (immutable). Ethereum allows everyone to deploy their own code, show each user the result and then agree that the code was executed correctly. The beauty of Etherium is that it is decentralised and populist. Ayone can use it to develop, trade and exchange. It is the medium most popular for creating and distributing #NFTs.
